# Privacy Policy for MyAssets

**Effective Date:** December 9, 2025  
**Last Updated:** December 9, 2025

## Introduction

MyAssets ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

**We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.**

MyAssets is designed with privacy as a core principle. All data you enter into the app remains exclusively on your device. We do not have access to your data, and it is never uploaded to our servers or any third-party services.

## Data Storage

All information you create or enter in MyAssets, including but not limited to:
- Asset records and details
- Receipt photos and images
- Warranty information
- Service history records
- Location and site information
- Retailer details

...is stored **locally on your device only**. This data is not synchronized to the cloud, not backed up to our servers, and is never transmitted over the internet to us or any third party.

## Permissions

MyAssets requests the following permissions to function properly:

### Camera Permission
- **Purpose:** To capture photos of receipts and asset documentation
- **Usage:** Photos are saved directly to your device storage and are not transmitted anywhere
- **Required:** Optional - only needed if you want to use the receipt photo feature

### Storage Permission (Read/Write External Storage)
- **Purpose:** To save and retrieve photos of receipts and asset images
- **Usage:** All files remain on your local device storage
- **Required:** Optional - only needed if you want to store receipt photos

### Internet Permission
- **Purpose:** License verification with Google Play Store to prevent piracy
- **Usage:** Only communicates with Google Play Services to verify legitimate app purchase
- **Data Transmitted:** Only license validation requests to Google Play (no personal data)
- **Required:** Yes - for app license verification

### Billing Permission
- **Purpose:** To verify the app was legitimately purchased from Google Play Store
- **Usage:** Communicates only with Google Play Services for license validation
- **Required:** Yes - for anti-piracy protection

## Third-Party Services

MyAssets uses the following third-party services:

### Google Play Services
- **Purpose:** License verification and app distribution
- **Data Shared:** Purchase verification only (handled by Google)
- **Privacy Policy:** [Google Play Services Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

We do **not** use:
- Analytics services
- Advertising networks
- Crash reporting services
- User tracking services
- Cloud storage or backup services

## Data Security

Since all your data is stored locally on your device:
- Your data security depends on your device's security measures
- We recommend using device encryption and secure lock screens
- You are responsible for backing up your data (we do not provide cloud backup)
- If you uninstall the app, all local data will be deleted

## Children's Privacy

MyAssets does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. Since we don't collect any data at all, the app is safe for users of all ages.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

## Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device:
- To delete all app data, simply uninstall the application
- Alternatively, clear app data through your device's Settings > Apps > MyAssets > Storage > Clear Data

## Your Rights

Under privacy regulations (such as GDPR, CCPA), you have rights regarding your personal data. However, since we do not collect, store, or process any of your personal data:
- We have no data to provide, modify, or delete
- All data control is in your hands via your device
- You can delete all data by uninstalling the app
